About US

Political Pen was founded on the dream of helping achieve freedom, prosperity and enlightenment in Lebanon and the Middle East. We strongly believe in the need to empower people and especially the youth to challenge rigid conventional political and social norms.

Who are we?

We are a group of young Lebanese, from different backgrounds and beliefs, dreaming of a better future for our region, our country, our families, and ourselves.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to expose the youth of the region to the values of democracy and liberty by making politics accessible, and convincing them that they too have a stake in whatâ€™s happening around them.

How do we do it?

By allowing healthy debate, new approaches and the spread of the unconventional. We show people that a lot of progress can be achieved by simply talking.
